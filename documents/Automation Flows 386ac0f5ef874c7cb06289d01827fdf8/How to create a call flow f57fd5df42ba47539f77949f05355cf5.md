# Steps to create a call flow

## Create a flow

### Load the flow builder

Navigate to the flow builder by clicking the ‚ÄúFlows‚Äù on the left side menu. Then when you have the flow page open you will see a link to ‚ÄúFlows‚Äù on the top of your page, please click that. Click on the ‚ÄòCreate Flow‚Äô button.

## Connect the flow to a number

### Open the phone numbers section

Please navigate the phone number section by clicking on the ‚ÄòPhone‚Äô on the left-side menu. When the page is open, click the ‚ÄòNumbers‚Äô button on the top menu.

![Untitled](../IN%20PROCESS%20Product%20FAQ%20Guides%20a986e24138d14caf8156bfe234b2e8fb/How%20to%20record%20inbound%20calls%202cd59d1357be439f872bef7facb37002/Untitled.png)

### Edit the phone number

Please, select your concerned phone number, and open it in ‚ÄòEdit‚Äô mode

![Untitled](../IN%20PROCESS%20Product%20FAQ%20Guides%20a986e24138d14caf8156bfe234b2e8fb/How%20to%20record%20inbound%20calls%202cd59d1357be439f872bef7facb37002/Untitled%201.png)

### Connect the flow

Please, select the ‚ÄòSend Inbound Phone Calls To‚Äô dropdown, and the system will provide you with a list containing existing call flows. Please, select your concerned flow.

![Untitled](../IN%20PROCESS%20Product%20FAQ%20Guides%20a986e24138d14caf8156bfe234b2e8fb/How%20to%20create%20a%20simple%20call%20flow%20for%20inbound%20calls%201e5d0b6cfd3548f6ba7d8e2ec94fac62/Untitled%207.png)

### Save the process

Now, click on the ‚ÄòSave‚Äô button to save the changes
